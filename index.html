Features
Everything you need to schedule smarter
From one-on-one meetings to team-wide scheduling, Skedvio handles it all without the back-and-forth.
🔗

Shareable Booking Links

Create a personal link and share it with anyone. They pick a time, you get notified. No account required for guests.

📅

Smart Calendar Sync

Connects with Google, Outlook, and Apple Calendar. Your availability updates automatically — always accurate.

⏰

Custom Availability

Set your working hours, buffer times, and blackout dates. Skedvio only shows times you actually want to meet.

🔔

Automated Reminders

Send automatic email or SMS reminders to reduce no-shows. Customize timing and message for each event type.

👥

Team Scheduling

Round-robin, collective availability, or let clients choose their preferred team member — all in one place.

📊

Analytics & Insights

See how your time is spent, track booking trends, and understand your most productive hours.
